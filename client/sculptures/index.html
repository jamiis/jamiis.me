Sculptures
As I wander about my life in various cities, and as I travel around the globe, I like to pick up old rusty bits of metal and glass, old machinery and trinkets, and anything else that looks interesting. I bring them home, I take them apart, and then I put them back together again.
Ned
Ned is an assemblage of clock and watch parts, and a wooden artists model. His insides are fully functional and can be turned using a pin located on his back. He currently resides in Chicago and is, like most people, more than the sum of his parts.
The Object
The Object is made of parts of several watches and clocks, a sewing machine, a stencil, a keyhole, and a copy of A Rose In Bloom by Louisa May Alcott
